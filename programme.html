Programme and Courses

Following Undergraduate and Postgraduate Courses offered in St.Xavier's College Ranchi

The college is an Autonomous College of Ranchi University. It offers Undergraduate (B.A./B.Com/B.Sc. Honours), Postgraduate (M.A./M.C.A./M.J.M.C) Courses under the faculty of Arts, Commerce & Science. Besides this it also offers Professional, Vocational & Management programmes.
